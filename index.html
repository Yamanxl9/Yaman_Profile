About me

Solving Complex Problems: Whether it is calculating packet delays in a network architecture, optimizing an image processing matrix, or transforming raw datasets into clean dashboards, I thrive on turning complex logic into structured, efficient solutions.

Continuous Growth:
I approach my academic career with an active hands-on mindset, frequently diving into hackathons, technical research, and personal dev projects to ensure my skills match the fast-moving pace of the tech industry.

Web Design & Accessibility: Dedicated to clean, front-end presentation. I build highly readable codebases and interfaces that prioritize the end-user experience without unnecessary bloat.
